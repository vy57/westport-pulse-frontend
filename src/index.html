About the Methodology

This engine connects directly to the FRED API for live mortgage rates and the US Census API (ACS 5-Year Estimates) for median household income and rent data.

It applies Connecticut's specific 70% assessed property tax rule against Westport's current 13.2 mill rate to provide hyper-localized, highly accurate macroeconomic insights that standard national calculators miss.
